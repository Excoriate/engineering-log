## Verdict

**NOT-READY**

The former cross-shell and literal-placeholder blockers are substantially repaired, but three reachable routes still fail one-shot execution: Branch B consumes unset state and has no commands, the admin-password route retains a dead verifier credential, and old-authorization identity is not safely discoverable. The incomplete permission and verification gates can also fail only after sensitive state changes or leave the executor unable to prove recovery.
